Pumpkin Whoopie Pie

What is a whoopie pie? Is it a cookie, pie, sandwich, or cake? All I know is that these things are scrumptious, especially in pumpkin season. Feel free to find out more about the whoopie pie.

  • Rating :

  • Prep Time : 5 Mins

  • Cook Time : 10 Mins

  • Ready In : 20 Mins

  • Category : Desert

  • Tags : Whoopie Pie, Pie, Cookie, Cake

Planning:

Ingredients

  • - 2 cups of Complete Pancake Mix

  • - 1 cup of canned pumpkin

  • - 1 Â½ cups Milk

  • - 2 TSP Cinnamon

  • - 1 TSP Nutmeg

  • - 1 TBS Vanilla

  • - 4 TBS butter or as needed for griddling

  • - 1 can of Cream Cheese Frosting



Preparation:

  • 1. In large bowl, gently whisk all ingredients until just combined.

  • 2. Let batter sit for at least 3-5 min.

Set your griddle to low medium heat (~375F)


Performance

Melt butter. Drop 1/8 cup of batter onto melted butter. Flip after 3 min (8 min total). Place on cooling rack to cool. Generously frost a pancake with the cream cheese frosting and place another on top to make a sandwich. This recipe makes about 24 pancakes/12 sandwiches.


Serve, enjoy!
