Egg Pamwich

My beautiful wife’s favorite sandwich, that she kindly shares with me. With eggs, bacon, sausage, and sharp cheddar cheese on griddled sourdough bread, what is not to like?

  • Rating :

  • Prep Time : 5 Mins

  • Cook Time : 5 Mins

  • Ready In : 10 Mins

  • Category : Breakfast

  • Tags : Breakfast, Egg, Sandwich, Bacon, Sausage

Planning:

Ingredients:

  • - 2 slices of Sourdough bread

  • - 4 slices of bacon

  • - 2 Sausage Patties

  • - 3 eggs, Scrambled

  • - 4 slices of sharp cheddar cheese

  • - 1 tsp butter/oil, or as needed for griddling



Preparation:

  • - Scramble three eggs into small container

  • - Butter bread on both sides, place in half sheet tray

  • - Place breakfast meats and cheese in half sheet tray

Set your griddle to medium heat (~400F) on one side, off on the other


Performance

Griddle breakfast meats on hot side of griddle flipping after 2 min (4 min total), shift over to cool side. Melt butter, pour eggs, flip and fold into a square. Griddle bread. Flip after 2 mins. Add 2 slices of cheese to each piece of bread and assemble sandwich.


Serve, enjoy!
